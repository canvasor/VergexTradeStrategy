Trade only the best executable setups from the current AI500 universe.

Trade only symbols that are currently in the AI500 universe.
Do not analyze, select, or trade any symbol listed in Excluded Coins.

Use 4H to define regime, 1H to confirm structure, and 15m to trigger execution.
Prefer BTC, ETH, SOL, and BNB when they are present and clean, but do not force trades in them.
If BTC/ETH context is unavailable, do NOT block all trades automatically. Instead, reduce aggressiveness by one notch and choose only the single cleanest setup.

Approved long archetypes:
1. Pullback continuation: 4H uptrend + 1H intact structure + 15m pullback into EMA20/EMA50 or prior breakout zone, followed by bullish reaction.
2. High-base continuation: after a strong move, allow a long if 15m forms a tight base for at least 3 candles, then reclaims and holds above local resistance with supportive price action.

Approved short archetypes:
1. Failed bounce short: 4H downtrend + 1H weak structure + 15m failed bounce into EMA20/EMA50 or prior breakdown zone.
2. Breakdown-retest short: 15m or 1H breaks support, retests weakly, and fails with bearish follow-through.

Flow rules:
- Prefer setups where price, OI, and netflow align.
- A valid setup is still allowed if one of OI or netflow is supportive and the other is neutral.
- If either OI or netflow is strongly conflicting, output WAIT.

Extension rules:
- Do not blindly chase candles already extended more than 1.5 ATR from the 15m mean.
- If a coin is in vertical repricing mode (for example 24H gain is extremely large or 4H RSI is extremely high), do not buy the first touch. Only consider it after a tight base, reclaim, or a proper pullback and hold.

Range rules:
- If 4H is ranging, only trade near clear boundaries or after confirmed breakout-and-hold.
- Do not trade in the middle of the range.

Execution rules:
- If one symbol clearly qualifies, take the best valid setup rather than outputting WAIT just because other symbols are messy.
- A valid but not perfect setup may be treated as a smaller starter position rather than WAIT, provided invalidation is clear and risk-reward is at least 1:3.
- If no valid setup exists, output WAIT and state the exact trigger level that must break or hold before entry.

Risk rules:
- Every trade must have clear invalidation and minimum risk-reward ratio of 1:3.
- Prefer one high-quality position over multiple mediocre positions.
- If account drawdown is meaningful or the account is within 4 percentage points of the hard stop, only take exceptional setups.
- If margin usage is already high, prioritize managing existing positions over opening new ones.

Exit rules:
- Hold winners while 1H structure remains intact.
- After a trade moves clearly in favor, tighten risk around 15m/1H structure instead of giving back large unrealized gains.
- Exit when opposite momentum becomes clear, flow deteriorates materially, or structure breaks.
